## The State of Telegram Bot Scams

### Why Have Bots Become New Targets?

Telegram trading bots (like GMGN, Banana Gun, Maestro) are popular among traders for their convenience:
- Quick new token sniping
- Auto-copy trading
- Gas optimization
- Multi-chain support

But this **convenience** is exploited by scammers:
- Users are accustomed to one-click wallet connections
- Frequent trading lowers vigilance
- Complex Bot interfaces make them hard to distinguish

## Common Scam Tactics

### Tactic 1: Fake GMGN Official Bot

**Method**:
1. Create fake Bots named "GMGN_Official_Bot" or "GMGN_Support"
2. "Randomly" discover your trading history in groups
3. Private message claiming "trading anomaly detected"
4. Induce you to "re-authorize" your wallet
5. Transfer all assets immediately after authorization

**Identification Points**:
- Real GMGN Bot username is @GMGNAIBot (fixed)
- Official will never proactively message users
- Will never ask for "re-authorization"

### Tactic 2: Fake Banana Gun Customer Service

**Method**:
1. Impersonate customer service in official groups
2. Claim to "resolve transaction failures"
3. Send "fix links" (phishing sites)
4. Induce input of seed phrases or private keys

**Identification Points**:
- Real Banana Gun has no "human customer service"
- All issues are documented in official docs
- Anyone asking for private keys is a scammer

### Red Flags 🚩

**STOP immediately** if you encounter:

- [ ] Bot proactively messages you
- [ ] Requests for private keys or seed phrases
- [ ] Asks to "reconnect" or "re-authorize"
- [ ] Sends suspicious links to click
- [ ] Promises "guaranteed profits" or "inside info"
- [ ] Username has subtle differences from official

## Best Practices for Safe Bot Usage

### Wallet Isolation Strategy

```
Recommended Configuration:

🔥 Dedicated Trading Wallet (Small Amount)
├─ For daily Bot trading
├─ Keep under 5% of total assets
└─ Regularly transfer profits to cold wallet

🔒 Savings Wallet (Large Amount)
├─ Hardware wallet storage
├─ Never connect to any Bot
└─ For long-term holding
```

### Pre-Use Security Checks

1. **Double-Verify Bot Identity**
   - Confirm Bot username through official Twitter
   - Cross-verify on official Discord/website

2. **Small Amount Test**
   - First use: transfer small amount for testing
   - Confirm normal trading and withdrawal work

3. **Permission Check**
   - Use revoke.cash to check authorizations
   - Regularly revoke unused Bot authorizations

## If You've Authorized a Fake Bot

### Emergency Damage Control (Within 5 Minutes)

1. **Immediately Revoke Authorization**
   - Visit revoke.cash
   - Connect wallet
   - Revoke all authorizations from suspicious Bots

2. **Transfer Remaining Assets**
   - Create new wallet with fresh address
   - Immediately transfer all assets
   - Discard the compromised wallet

3. **Save Evidence**
   - Screenshot fake Bot username and conversation
   - Record transaction hashes
   - Save phishing links
